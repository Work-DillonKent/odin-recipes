Lasagna

Description

This is an easy Lasagna dish that should be able to feed the entire family. Cooking this wonderful dish should only take 1hr20mins and produce 12 servings.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 (32 oz) jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 32 oz of cottage cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • salt of taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup water
